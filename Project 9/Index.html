My Personal Interests

When I'm not studying or completing an assignment for school, I like to go to the gym and exercise. It's always a good release for me when I'm feeling stressed or just stuck infront of a computer all day. It also brings a sense of achievement when you hit your goals.

Working out also allows me to balance my eating habits. I thoroughly love to cook and try new foods. There's nothing better than cooking a meal for people and knowing they're enjoying it.
